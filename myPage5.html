The Gods

Nordic gods live in the realm of Asgard. The gods are separated into 2 different clans: Aesir & Vanir. The first clan holds the main gods while the second clan holds the fertility gods. It was necessary for the two families to combine their powers and ideals for all to prosper.


Here are a few gods listed below:

God/Goddess Role Clan
Odin Leader of the Aesir and the god of war, wisdom, magic, and poetry. Aesir
Freya Goddess of love, sex, lust, beauty, sorcery, fertility, gold, war and death. Vanir
Thor Son of Odin and the god of thunder and lightning. Aesir
Njord Father of Freya and the god of wealth, fertility, the sea, and seafaring. Vanir
Baldur The god of light, joy, purity, and the summer sun. Aesir
Nerthus The goddess of fertility and an incarnation of the Mother Earth. Vanir
